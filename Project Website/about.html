About Us

Christine Chan established the RnE shop. The RnE is an abbreviation of her husband's name, "Royce," and her son's name, Ezekiel. She was inspired to start this business since shopping is her favorite hobby and she also has a child to raise. She dreamt of many things for her child, but due to financial constraints, she was unable to fulfill them. So that's where she gets the idea of starting up a business.
